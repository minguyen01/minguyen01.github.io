About

My name is Minh Nguyen and I grew up in San Francisco, California. I am currently a third-year Computer Science major at the University of California, Irvine who aspires to be a software engineer that not only creates reliable and robust programs, but also clear and clean UI design.

Background

I grew up in San Francisco, California for a large part of my life. I lived in the Tenderloin neighborhood but due to it being a relatively high-crime area, it was not the most pleasant part of the city to live in. However, I found myself to work closely with the community there through the non-profit organizations in the area. My experiences from college includes planning/managing events with up to 300+ attendees, working in a team where important decisions with tradeoffs must be made, and designing engaging posters for the clubs I was involved in.

Interests

While computer science remains the main area of study throughout my education, I am also interested in graphics designing. I took a lot of graphics design courses in both middle-school and high-school, and it is something that I also enjoyed doing a lot. I was able to improve and put this skill to use by creating posters, flyers, and t-shirts for the various clubs that I was involved in. One of my hobbies that I am most committed to is break-dancing, which I started during high-school and later became one of the coordinators for the break-dancing team in UCI during my third year in college. I also like bingeing punk rock albums, with some of my favorite bands being Thrice, Silverstein, and Rise Against.
